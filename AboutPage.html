Who

Ben is an aspiring developer and student at the University of Alabama. Majoring in Computer Science and Japanese, he is motivated to partake in a variety of hobbies, including photography, coding, tennis, and traveling. Ben recently traveled to Japan, where he represented the United States as a Youth Ambassador at the 2025 Osaka-Kansai Expo. During his time in Japan, Ben was able to see his hobbies through a new lens, no pun intended, and capture moments that reflect his unique perspective. This whole website was created by Ben himself, showcasing not only his photography and skills in web development, but also his conviction to project his passions onto the world.
